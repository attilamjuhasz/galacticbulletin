What is Galactic Cannibalism?


When galaxies collide, they interact through gravity, creating dramatic changes and new stars. They scatter the celestial bodies they hold within them and sometimes even create whole new galaxies. For that to happen though, they must be roughly the same size; otherwise, one would just consume the other.
