About

Hello! I'm Attila Juhasz, a sophomore in the Montgomery County School District and the creator of this website, along with all the blogs it hosts. During my freshman summer, I read a book called A Brief History of Time by Stephen Hawking, and I fell in love with topics that I had previously thought were science fiction but turned out to be theoretically possible. This created a desire within me to read more books and learn about the mysteries of the cosmos. I soon realized that without that first book to spark my love for astrophysics, I would never have known it was my calling. That's why I created this website: to write blogs, not just because I enjoy writing, but to inspire others with reading, just as it inspired me.
